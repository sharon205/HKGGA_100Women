前言 | Preface

香港女童軍運動始於1916年，肩負「讓女孩子和青年婦女發揮她們的潛能，成為有責任感的世界公民」的使命。我們的願景是：為青少年提供優質的非正規教育，憑藉我們一群充滿熱誠的領袖，讓更多女孩子及青年婦女可以透過女童軍的訓練，為自己及身邊所有人建立一個美好而積極的人生。

一百年以來，女童軍運動培育了不少品格優秀、知識技能豐富、身心健康、切實服務社會的人士。適逢香港女童軍總會慶祝百周年會慶，我們收集了100位曾經或現役的女童軍會員，她們與大家一同分享故事，了解女童軍的教育及活動對她們在成長路上的改變。

The Hong Kong Girl Guides Association has been established since 1916. Its mission is to create a platform for girls and young women to exhibit their talents so to become responsible global citizens.

We also have the vision that in the education perspective Guiding Movement can provide the youth a whole person personality education which is based on the basic principal and spiritual aspects of the World Association of Girl Guides and Girl Scouts such as, consensus building, enhancing unity, aiming at global friendship, promoting world peace and so as to build up a fabulous and positive life for girls and the people around.

Besides, for the past hundred years, a lot of enthusiastic people have taken part in the Guiding Movement. Therefore, a lot of girls guides have been trained and nourished with rich knowledgeable skills, healthy mind and attitude. They also participate actively in community services.

This year, coincidently, is the Centenary Celebration of the Hong Kong Girl Guides Association, we want to collect 100 affiliated girl guides’ stories, in order to study how Guiding Movement and education inspires their growth.
